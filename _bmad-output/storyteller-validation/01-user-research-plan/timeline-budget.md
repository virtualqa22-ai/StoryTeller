# Timeline & Budget

## Week 1: Preparation
- **Day 1-2:** Create Figma prototypes (3 prototypes, interactive)
- **Day 3:** Write interview scripts and screening questions
- **Day 4:** Create recruitment screener (Google Forms)
- **Day 5:** Post recruitment ads across all channels
- **Ongoing:** Screen applicants, send confirmation emails

**Deliverables:** Prototypes, scripts, recruitment posts

## Week 2-3: Recruitment & Scheduling
- **Ongoing:** Screen 50-100 applicants (target 20 qualified)
- **Ongoing:** Schedule 20 x 60-minute interviews
- **Target:** 2-3 interviews per day over 2 weeks
- **Ongoing:** Send confirmation emails with Zoom links and consent forms

**Deliverables:** 20 scheduled interviews

## Week 3-4: Interviews
- **Day 1-10:** Conduct 20 x 60-minute interviews (2 per day)
- **Ongoing:** Record and transcribe (with participant consent)
- **Ongoing:** Take detailed notes during interviews
- **End of week:** Send $75 incentives to participants

**Deliverables:** 20 interview recordings + transcripts

## Week 5: Analysis
- **Day 1-2:** Synthesize findings across all interviews
- **Day 3:** Create validated persona profiles
- **Day 4:** Analyze feature rankings, create value matrix
- **Day 5:** Analyze pricing data, create Van Westendorp chart
- **Day 6-7:** Write UX validation report

**Deliverables:** All 5 deliverables listed above

## Week 6: Recommendations
- **Day 1-2:** Present findings to team
- **Day 3-4:** Update UX specification based on research
- **Day 5:** Make strategic decisions (persona priority, pricing model, MVP scope)
- **Day 6-7:** Finalize MVP specification and implementation plan

**Deliverables:** Strategic decision document, final MVP spec
