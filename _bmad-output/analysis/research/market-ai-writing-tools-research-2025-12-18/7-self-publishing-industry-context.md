# 7. Self-Publishing Industry Context

## 7.1 Self-Publishing Market Size

**Market Growth:** ([Automateed](https://www.automateed.com/self-publishing-statistics))
- **2024 Market Size**: $1.85 billion
- **2033 Projection**: $5.55+ billion (market will triple)
- **CAGR**: 16.7% (2025-2033)

**Publishing Volume:** ([Publishers Weekly](https://www.publishersweekly.com/pw/by-topic/industry-news/publisher-news/article/96468-self-publishing-s-output-and-infuence-continue-to-grow.html))
- **2023**: 2.6 million self-published titles (with ISBNs)
- **Growth**: 7.2% increase over 2022
- Self-published titles now **outnumber traditional publishing by 2M+ annually**

## 7.2 Amazon KDP Dominance

**Market Share & Revenue:** ([Automateed](https://www.automateed.com/self-publishing-statistics))
- Amazon KDP: **65-70% market share**
- **1.4 million titles** released annually through KDP alone
- **87% of indie authors** name Amazon as top revenue source ([Written Word Media](https://www.writtenwordmedia.com/2024-indie-author-survey-results-insights-into-self-publishing-for-authors/))
- **87.5%** have books enrolled in Kindle Unlimited

**Author Earnings:** ([Automateed](https://www.automateed.com/self-publishing-statistics), [ZonGuru](https://www.zonguru.com/blog/how-much-do-self-published-authors-make-on-amazon))
- Average: **$150-$20,000+ per month** on Kindle
- Median indie author income: **$12,759/year** (2023, up 53% YoY)
- Total author earnings: **$874M in ebook sales** (2022)
- KDP royalties paid: **$3.5 billion over 10 years** ($650M in last 12 months)

## 7.3 Author Economics

**Publishing Costs:** ([Alliance of Independent Authors](https://www.allianceindependentauthors.org/facts/))
- **Average cost to publish**: $2,940-$5,660 per book (2025)
- Editing: $500-3,000
- Cover design: $200-1,500
- Formatting: $200-500 (or $250 for Vellum)
- Marketing: $500-2,000+

**Author Productivity:** ([Written Word Media](https://www.writtenwordmedia.com/2024-indie-author-survey-results-insights-into-self-publishing-for-authors/))
- Average books published: **8 books per author**
- Genre authors produce **2-4 books per year** (high performers)

**StoryTeller Value Proposition:**
- Eliminate formatting costs ($200-500 per book)
- Reduce editing costs through AI consistency (10-20 hours saved)
- Faster time to market (critical for genre authors)
- ROI after 1-2 books for typical author
