# 11. Economic Analysis: Author Costs & Revenue

## 11.1 Publishing Cost Breakdown

**Average Production Costs Per Book:** ([Alliance of Independent Authors](https://www.allianceindependentauthors.org/facts/))

**Total Range:** $2,940 - $5,660 per book (2025 data from Reedsy)

**Component Breakdown:**
- **Editing**: $500 - $3,000 (developmental, copy, line, proof)
- **Cover Design**: $200 - $1,500 (professional designer)
- **Formatting**: $200 - $500 (or $250 for Vellum one-time)
- **Marketing**: $500 - $2,000+ (ads, promotions, materials)
- **ISBN** (optional): $125 (single), $295 (10-pack) US; free in Canada
- **Miscellaneous**: Beta readers, sensitivity readers, legal review

**Cost Reduction Opportunities for StoryTeller:**
- **Eliminate formatting costs**: $200-500 per book saved with built-in export
- **Reduce editing costs**: AI consistency reduces developmental editing needs (est. 10-20 hours saved = $300-700)
- **ROI threshold**: Tool pays for itself after 1-2 books

## 11.2 Author Revenue Analysis

**Median Author Income:** ([Alliance of Independent Authors](https://www.allianceindependentauthors.org/facts/))
- **2023**: $12,759/year (median self-publisher income)
- **Growth**: Up 53% year-over-year
- **Disparity**: Wide range from <$1,000 to $100,000+

**Amazon KDP Earnings:** ([ZonGuru](https://www.zonguru.com/blog/how-much-do-self-published-authors-make-on-amazon), [Automateed](https://www.automateed.com/self-publishing-statistics))
- **Monthly range**: $150 (lower end) to $20,000+ (higher end)
- **10-year KDP royalties**: $3.5 billion paid to authors total
- **Recent 12 months**: $650 million in royalties
- **2022 ebook sales**: $874 million captured by self-published authors

**Books Published Per Author:** ([Written Word Media](https://www.writtenwordmedia.com/2024-indie-author-survey-results-insights-into-self-publishing-for-authors/))
- **Average**: 8 books per author
- **High performers** (earning $50K+/year): 15-30+ books

**Revenue Model:**
- **Volume business**: More books = more discoverability = more income
- **Backlist value**: Older books continue earning (long tail)
- **Series advantage**: Read-through multiplies revenue per reader
