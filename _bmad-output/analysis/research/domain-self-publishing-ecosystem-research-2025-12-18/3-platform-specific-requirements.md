# 3. Platform-Specific Requirements

## 3.1 Amazon KDP File Requirements

**eBook Formats (as of March 2025):** ([MK Williams](https://1mkwilliams.com/2025/06/25/kindle-create-tutorial-convert-ms-word-to-epub-or-kpf-what-files-will-amazon-kdp-accept-for-ebook/), [LivingWriter](https://livingwriter.com/blog/how-to-publish-a-book-on-amazon-2025-kdp-guide/))

**Accepted Formats:**
- **EPUB** (recommended - standard since 2022)
- **KPF** (Kindle Package Format - from Kindle Create tool)
- **DOC/DOCX** (acceptable for text-only books)
- **⚠️ MOBI no longer accepted** (dropped March 2025)

**Format Recommendations:**
- **Text-only books**: Word files acceptable
- **Tables or images**: EPUB or KPF recommended
- **Best quality**: EPUB (reflowable, adapts to screen sizes)

**Paperback Formats:** ([KDP Help](https://kdp.amazon.com/en_US/help/topic/G201857950))
- **PDF** (without bleed or with bleed)
- **DOC, DOCX, RTF, HTML, TXT** (auto-converted to PDF)

## 3.2 Cover Design Requirements

**eBook Covers:** ([KDP Guidelines](https://kdp.amazon.com/en_US/help/topic/G6GTK3T3NUHKLEFX), [Book Cover Hub](https://bookcoverhub.com/blog/book-cover-design-for-amazon/))

**Technical Specs:**
- **Minimum resolution**: 300 DPI (dots per inch)
- **Dimensions**: 6:9 ratio (standard)
- **Minimum size**: 1000 pixels on longest side
- **Recommended**: 2560 x 1600 pixels for quality
- **Format**: JPEG for eBooks
- **File size**: Maximum 650MB

**Content Requirements:**
- Must include Title and Author/Contributor information
- Text should be at least **7-point font** (readable)
- **No crop marks, color bars, or template text**

**Paperback Covers:** ([KDP Help](https://kdp.amazon.com/en_US/help/topic/G201953020))
- **Single PDF file**: Includes back cover, spine, front cover (one image)
- **All images**: Minimum 300 DPI
- **Flatten transparencies**: Prevents printing errors
- **Embed fonts**: Ensures correct rendering
- **Spine text**: Requires at least 79 pages

**Cover Finish Options:**
- **Glossy**: Shiny, typical for textbooks, children's books, nonfiction
- **Matte**: Minimal sheen, typical for novels and fiction
- **Paper**: 80 pound (220 GSM) white stock
