# 10. Author Communities & Support

## 10.1 Major Online Communities

**Top Writing Communities:** ([Pepper Content](https://www.pepper.inc/blog/writing-communities/), [Lulu Blog](https://blog.lulu.com/7-online-writing-communities-for-authors/), [TCK Publishing](https://www.tckpublishing.com/online-writing-clubs/))

### **Scribophile**
- **Size**: "One of the **largest communities** in the world"
- **Features**: Workshops, tutorials, critique exchange
- **Audience**: Writers of all skill levels, all genres
- **Value**: Peer feedback, professional development

### **She Writes**
- **Size**: "Over **34,000 members**"
- **Audience**: "**Largest writing community** exclusively for women"
- **Content**: Articles on writing, editing, promoting, marketing

### **Critique Circle**
- **Features**: User forum + peer-to-peer critique section
- **Resources**: Free resources on metaphors, magic systems, character development
- **Activity**: "Very active community"

### **The Insecure Writer's Support Group**
- **Mission**: Help writers overcome self-doubt and insecurities
- **Focus**: Emotional support, mental health
- **Value**: Addresses "#1 factor holding back" publication ([Happy Self-Publisher](https://happyselfpublisher.com/tackling-the-10-pain-points-of-publishing/))

### **Reddit Writing Communities**
- **Scale**: "Countless 'subreddits'"
- **Variety**: Genre-specific threads, critique groups, resource sharing
- **Benefit**: "Niche threads" for every interest

### **The Next Big Writer**
- **Type**: International forum for feedback exchange
- **Cost**: $8.95/month
- **Value**: "Thorough critiques from published authors"

## 10.2 NaNoWriMo (National Novel Writing Month)

**Overview:** ([Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/National_Novel_Writing_Month), [Pepper Content](https://www.pepper.inc/blog/writing-communities/))

**Description:**
- "One of the **most famous online writing communities**"
- "Writers across the globe who support and encourage writers to upskill"
- **Writing forums**: "Some of the best on the web for authors"
- Genre-specific advice, Coffee House social area

**2025 Update:**
- **⚠️ Organization closure**: "In March 2025, director announced **closure of the nonprofit**"
- **Reason**: "Citing financial struggles and 'community vitriol'"
- **Impact**: Forums may shut down, but event tradition continues

**Value (Historical):**
- November writing challenge: 50,000 words in 30 days
- Community accountability and motivation
- Resource library for participants

## 10.3 Community Value Propositions

**Why Authors Join Communities:** ([Lulu Blog](https://blog.lulu.com/7-online-writing-communities-for-authors/))

1. **"Find supportive friends"**
2. **"Get feedback"** on work in progress
3. **"Swap writing tips"**
4. **"Learn firsthand** from authors who've been through publishing process"

**Pain Point Addressed:**
- Writing is solitary profession
- Isolation can lead to self-doubt
- Community provides accountability and validation
